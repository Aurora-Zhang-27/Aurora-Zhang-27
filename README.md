# Hi, I'm Aurora Zhang

I'm an Honors Statistics student at the University of Alberta, with a minor in Computing Science.  
I work on machine learning, time-series forecasting, and carbon-aware energy systems.

My current research focuses on structured short-term grid carbon-intensity forecasting through fuel-mix surrogates, with applications in carbon-aware operation, uncertainty diagnostics, and energy decision analytics.
